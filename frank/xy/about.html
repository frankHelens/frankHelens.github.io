About Us

Shining CG is a creative corporation that specializes in portraying unbuilt architecture. We help the architect and the owners to convert building still in the design stage, into visual images or video, show the essence of complete building.

Since the year of 1998, we have finished more than 20000 3D visualization creativities of all kinds of architecture for more than 2000 different clients coming from 18 countries of 5 continents. We have used 3D technology, shooting, drawing and architectonic to provide the clients with the services of CG rendering, CG animation, BIM and 3D products finally
